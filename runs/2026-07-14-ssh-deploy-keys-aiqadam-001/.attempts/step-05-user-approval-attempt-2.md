Solution-designer proposed three options and recommended: create a dedicated per-environment group (`aiqadam-qa-secrets`, `aiqadam-prod-secrets`), add both `tvolodi` (existing owner) and `deploy` to it, `chgrp` each `.env` to that group, `chmod 640` — owner unchanged, only group/mode bits change, no secret value is read, logged, or regenerated.
